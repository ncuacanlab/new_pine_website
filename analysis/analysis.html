Analysis

The analyst enters the account with password in PINE and switches to the analysis page.

STEP 1:

Select parameters in the training data tab and press the search button.

STEP 2:

Select parameters in the “label data” tab and press the “search” button to complete the data selection

STEP 3:

Press “Next” to the Preprocessing step. Selecting the method, press the process button. The results of the respective data after the preprocessing will be displayed.

STEP 4:

Then press “Next” to the “Training” step. Set the data ratio of “train” and “validation” and what method to use for segmenting. Select category, layer, “batch_size" and “epoch”. After the selection, four fields (layer + output layer) will be displayed, the first three layers can be set to “neuron_num”, “activation” and “dropout”, and the fourth layer can be set to “output_num” and “activation”. Press the “train” button. Pressing the “analDownload” button, the trained model can be analDownloaded.

STEP 5:

The results of the model training process, such as validation loss and validation accuracy, are displayed.
